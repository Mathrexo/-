Algerians daily search for doctores The Algeria Medicine Platform is a website created by Dhyaa LA and Mouadh MKH. This platform is dedicated to helping Algerians with their daily search for doctors and medical services across the country. It provides an easy-to-use interface where users can: Search for doctors by specialty, location, or name View detailed profiles, including contact information, clinic hours, and patient reviews Book appointments directly through the website (if supported) Discover nearby pharmacies, clinics, and hospitals The platform aims to make healthcare more accessible and organized for all Algerians, whether they are in major cities or remote regions.

The Algeria Doctors Platform is an informative website created by Dhyaa LA and Mouadh MKH to help users easily access data about doctors practicing in Algeria. It offers a simple and modern interface where visitors can search for doctors by name, specialty, location, and availability. The platform aims to improve transparency and assist both professionals and the public in finding accurate medical information. It stands out as a digital step toward better healthcare access and professional visibility in the Algerian system.

Work way

Work way: 🔍 1. Choose the Illness or Specialty Use the search bar or category menu to select the type of illness or the medical specialty you need (e.g. cardiology, dermatology, pediatrics). 👨‍⚕️ 2. Browse Doctors List The platform shows a list of doctors who specialize in the selected field. Each doctor is displayed with a profile card. 📞 3. View Doctor Details Click on a doctor to see more information, including: Full name Phone number 📱 Location on the map 📍 Working hours 🕒 🗺️ 4. Locate the Doctor An interactive map will show you the exact geographic location of the doctor’s clinic or hospital, making it easy to plan your visit.
